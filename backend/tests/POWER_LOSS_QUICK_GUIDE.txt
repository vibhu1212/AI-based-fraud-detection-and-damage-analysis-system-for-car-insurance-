║                    🔌 POWER LOSS RECOVERY - QUICK GUIDE                      ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

📋 WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW:

  ✅ YOLOv8 saves checkpoints AUTOMATICALLY after every epoch
  ✅ Maximum loss: ONE epoch (~20-30 minutes)
  ✅ Resume is AUTOMATIC and EASY

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🚀 TO RESUME TRAINING AFTER POWER LOSS:

  1. Turn on your computer
  2. Open terminal
  3. Navigate to project:
     cd ~/Desktop/car\ detection

  4. Run resume script:
     backend/venv/bin/python backend/scripts/resume_training.py

  That's it! Training will continue from the last completed epoch.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📁 CHECKPOINT LOCATIONS:

  Checkpoints are saved here:
  runs/detect/backend/runs/damage_detection/yolov8m_damage_v12/weights/
  
  Files:
  • last.pt  - Most recent completed epoch
  • best.pt  - Best validation performance so far

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

⚠️  SPECIAL CASES:

  If power loss happened during FIRST epoch (before any checkpoint):
  → You'll need to restart training from scratch:
    backend/venv/bin/python backend/scripts/train_yolo_damage.py
  
  If checkpoint is corrupted:
  → Use best.pt instead of last.pt (see full guide)
